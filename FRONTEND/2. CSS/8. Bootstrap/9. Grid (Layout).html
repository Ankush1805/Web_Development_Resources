Vertical Alignment

align-items-start

align-items-start: Aligns items to the start of the container's cross axis (top)
align-items-center: Centers items along the container's cross axis.
align-items-end: Aligns items to the end of the container's cross axis (bottom).
align-items-baseline: Aligns items to their baseline.
align-items-stretch: Stretches items to fill the container's cross axis.
